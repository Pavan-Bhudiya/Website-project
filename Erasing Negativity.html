ERASING NEGATIVITY

So of late I've noticed that many guys have the wrong attitude esp when approaching a hurdle in their life. Since I'm still a student I've narrowed down to a very small example. In our school by end of 3rd term of f1 we have to choose which subjects we wanna retain and continue to learn and those which we wanna drop. Well I know my fellow students must be thinking by now that what's hard in that, just choose the subject u perform in well, the one u enjoy learning etc. . The most queer reasons I heard was just choose the 1 with the teacher u like and choose the one ur bestie is taking. Like seriously, how can those reasons or should I say EXCUSE be used to judge which subject ull take, is that even normal?

Looking keenly into the matter, it may seem an easy job to choose subjects on the above mentioned basis, but have u ever sat down and thought by urself that now this small mere decision can play a key role in deciding the career u will pick later on. A small wrong step can change the direction of ur career. I'm guessing by now my fellow stude's must be like what's  this ''over-serious'' and over-ambtioned dude telling us ati choosing subjects wrongly can mess up my life and career? Bullshit!!(P.S. no offensiveness meant). Imagine if u aspired to be a lawyer and u took a wrong decision now and took geo instead of history or u wanted to become an entrepreneur and took comps instead of business, ull regret it later on in life wont u?
